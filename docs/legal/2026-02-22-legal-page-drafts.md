# Deliverable C — Marketing Red-Flag List (Claims to Avoid)

The following claims or phrasings should NOT appear on Margin Invest marketing pages, landing pages, emails, social media, or any user-facing copy. Each creates regulatory risk, user expectation risk, or both.

### Absolute Performance Claims
1. **"Guaranteed returns"** or **"guaranteed profits"** — No investment outcome can be guaranteed. Ever.
2. **"You will make money"** / **"You will beat the market"** — Implies certainty of positive outcomes.
3. **"Risk-free"** or **"no-risk investing"** — All investing involves risk. This is misleading.
4. **"Our picks always outperform"** — "Always" is indefensible. Past outperformance doesn't guarantee future.
5. **"X% annual returns"** without prominent backtest/hypothetical disclaimers — Specific return figures need context.

### Advice & Recommendation Language
6. **"We recommend buying/selling [stock]"** — This is investment advice. You are not registered to give it.
7. **"Our top picks"** or **"Stocks to buy now"** — Implies recommendation or solicitation. Use "highest-scoring" or "top-rated by our model" instead.
8. **"Trust our analysis"** / **"Rely on our scores"** — Contradicts your own disclaimer that users should not rely solely on the platform.
9. **"Expert-backed"** / **"Professionally managed"** — Implies advisory expertise or portfolio management.
10. **"All you need to invest"** — Implies replacement for professional advice and due diligence.

### Regulatory & Status Misrepresentations
11. **"SEC-approved"** / **"FINRA-approved"** / **"Regulated platform"** — You hold no registrations or approvals. These agencies do not "approve" analysis tools.
12. **"Licensed investment tool"** — Implies a license you don't hold.
13. **"Fiduciary"** or **"acting in your best interest"** — You are not a fiduciary.

### Misleading Backtest / Performance Framing
14. **Showing backtest results without "hypothetical" label** — All backtested performance must be clearly labeled as such.
15. **"Proven track record"** — Backtested results are not a track record. Only live, audited results qualify.
16. **"Exposed the market's biggest winners before they moved"** — Implies predictive power your disclaimers explicitly deny.
17. **Cherry-picking best-performing examples without showing full distribution** — Survivorship bias. Show the distribution or don't show individual examples.

### AI Overclaiming
18. **"AI-powered investment advice"** — Combines "AI" with "advice," creating both AI hype risk and advisory language risk.
19. **"Our AI predicts market movements"** — Implies predictive certainty that doesn't exist.
20. **"Smarter than Wall Street"** / **"Beats the pros"** — Unsubstantiated comparative claims.

### General Overstatements
21. **"Never miss a winner"** / **"Catch every opportunity"** — Implies completeness and certainty.
22. **"Passive income from stocks"** — Implies guaranteed income generation.
23. **"Financial freedom"** / **"Quit your job"** — Lifestyle claims tied to investment outcomes are high-risk.
